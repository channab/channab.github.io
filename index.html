SmartUp Online Learning Platform

SmartUp is an online learning platform where organizations can buy subscriptions and create & manage organizational contents. SmartUp supports various kinds of content such as text, images, videos, quizes,surveys, embeded videos. Employees of the organization can follow the assigned content and they can interact with contents via liking, commenting and sharing. One intersting feature of the system is users can earn points based on the contents they have completed. There is a leadeboard which shows points of the users and users can convert their points to voucher points, from which they can buy vouchers from voucher platform integrated with SmartUp.

OneChat Mobile App

The issue with the public IMs for the corporates is data is communicated through a third party channel , so security of the messages are a concern for some of the corporate organizations. OneChat is the perfect solution for organizations who consider security is more important. It allows them manage chat infrastructure in their own premises and can be scaled based on the organizational requirements.

Inventory Management System

IMS solution is an inventory management system for HP Inc, APAC region. The solution provides many functionalities such as recieving purchase orders, move inventory between warehouses, scrapping, reverse GR, print lables, inventory reports.

PowerUp! Mobile App

PoweUp is a mobile app develop for corporate employees at DBS bank. It is a coporate social networking app which enables users to browse coporate articles, jobs, announcements, learnings and also users can follow other users and endorse users. Users can share, comment and like on the content.

WEMP Portal

WEMP portal is a water management portal developed for PUB, Singapore. It allows coporates to upload their water efficiency plan for the next year and data is collected through installed meters from the client premises. If the water usage is deviated from the planned usage, system will notify users to take necessary actions.
